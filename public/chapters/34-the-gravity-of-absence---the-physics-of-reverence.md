The journey into this higher art begins by unlearning the desperate need for connection. The opposite, a posture of total detachment, is equally flawed; it is a brittle defense that isolates and stagnates. This is not the coldness of apathy; it is the quiet, unshakable confidence that your well-being is generated internally.
